We take your privacy seriously. Tascli only collects information necessary to provide safe, reliable transportation in Utah Valley. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

Tascli is a rideshare platform connecting passengers with trusted local drivers in Utah Valley and surrounding areas. We are operated by July Twenty Enterprises LLC, a Utah limited liability company doing business as "Tascli." Our platform is accessible at tascli.com.

2. Information We Collect

From Passengers

  • Contact information: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Location data: Pickup and drop-off addresses you provide at booking
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details
  • Booking history: Ride dates, times, assigned drivers, fare amounts
  • Communications: Messages via app, WhatsApp (801-618-2397), or email
  • Family/caregiver data: If booking for a family member, their name and contact for safety

From Drivers

  • Identity documents: Driver's license, government-issued ID
  • Background check results: Via approved third-party providers
  • Vehicle information: Make, model, year, plate, insurance
  • Banking information: For weekly Stripe direct deposit
  • Profile photo and bio: Displayed to passengers for trust and transparency

Automatically Collected

  • Device type, browser, IP address
  • Pages visited and features used on tascli.com

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To match passengers with available verified drivers
  • To process payments and issue driver payouts via Stripe
  • To send ride confirmations and safety alerts via SMS and WhatsApp
  • To verify driver identity and run background checks
  • To resolve disputes or safety incidents
  • To comply with Utah and federal transportation regulations
  • To improve our platform and service quality

4. Minor Passengers — Special Protections

For passengers under 18, we require written parental consent, emergency contact, and school authorization where applicable. This data is handled with maximum care and never shared beyond the assigned driver.

Tascli does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents or legal guardians create and manage accounts on behalf of minors.

5. Payment Processing — Stripe

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified). Card data goes directly to Stripe — Tascli never stores, sees, or processes your full card number. Driver payouts are issued weekly via Stripe Connect. By using our payment features, you agree to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We share only when necessary:

  • With your assigned driver: Name, pickup location, phone for ride coordination
  • With passengers: Driver name, photo, vehicle, and rating for identification
  • With Stripe: Payment processing and driver payouts
  • With background check providers: Driver verification only
  • With law enforcement: When legally required or to protect safety

7. Data Retention

Ride records and account data are retained for 3 years for tax and regulatory compliance. Driver verification records for 5 years. You may request deletion by emailing [email protected] — some records may be kept for legal compliance.

8. Your Rights (Utah Consumer Privacy Act)

  • Know what personal data we collect and how we use it
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal holds)
  • Opt out of any future data sharing (we currently do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights: [email protected]

9. Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption, secure password practices, and limited access controls. No internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage strong, unique passwords for your Tascli account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of Tascli after that date constitutes acceptance.
