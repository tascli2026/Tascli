1. About Tascli

Tascli is a technology platform operated by July Twenty Enterprises LLC (EIN: 85-1937601), a Utah LLC doing business as "Tascli," that connects passengers with independent local drivers in Utah Valley and surrounding areas. Tascli is a marketplace — we are not a transportation company. Drivers are independent contractors, not employees.

2. Eligibility & Accounts

Passengers

  • Must be 18 years or older to create an account
  • Parents/guardians may book rides for minors (see Section 5)
  • Must provide accurate name, phone, and payment information
  • One account per person — non-transferable

Drivers

  • Must be 21 or older with a valid Utah driver's license
  • Must pass a Tascli-approved background check
  • Must maintain valid vehicle registration and rideshare insurance
  • Must be individually approved by Tascli before accepting rides
  • Are independent contractors — not employees of July Twenty Enterprises LLC

Drivers are independent contractors. Tascli does not control their schedules, routes, or methods. Drivers are responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and vehicle expenses.

3. Passenger Terms

As a passenger you agree to:

  • Be ready at pickup location at the scheduled time
  • Treat drivers with respect — harassment or threats result in immediate account termination
  • Not bring weapons, illegal substances, or open alcohol containers
  • Wear a seatbelt at all times during the ride
  • Provide accurate pickup and destination information
  • Leave honest reviews — fake or retaliatory reviews are prohibited

Passengers with a rating below 3.0 may be suspended. Drivers may cancel rides for intoxicated, aggressive, or non-compliant passengers.

4. Driver Terms & Commission

As an approved Tascli driver you agree to:

  • Maintain a clean, roadworthy vehicle at all times
  • Carry valid rideshare insurance during all Tascli trips
  • Arrive at pickup within the agreed time window
  • Never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Follow all Utah traffic laws
  • Never share your driver account with another person
  • Maintain a driver rating of 4.0 or above
  • Report any accidents within 24 hours to [email protected]

Commission Structure

Tascli retains 15% of the gross fare as a platform fee. Drivers receive 85% of each fare, paid weekly via Stripe direct deposit. Commission rates may be updated with 30 days written notice.

5. Minor Passengers — Special Terms

Rides for passengers under 18 require explicit written parental consent before each ride. No exceptions.

  • A parent or guardian must create the account and book all rides for minors
  • Written consent via in-app confirmation is required per ride
  • An emergency contact must be provided
  • Drivers assigned to minors have passed enhanced background checks
  • Drivers will never leave a minor unattended at drop-off
  • If no adult is present at drop-off, driver will contact the emergency contact and wait

6. Pricing & Fixed Fares

Tascli uses a zone-based fixed pricing model. Prices are calculated based on destination zone within Utah County, ride type, and any agreed extras. The fixed fare is displayed and confirmed before booking.

Tascli does not use surge pricing. The price you see at booking is the price you pay — always. No hidden fees, no multipliers.

Base Zone Pricing (from Provo)

  • Zone 1 — Provo/Orem core: $25–45
  • Zone 2 — Utah Valley: $40–60
  • Zone 3 — Extended Utah County: $55–75
  • SLC Airport flat rate: $60–70

Add-ons

  • Wait time at appointment: +$15
  • Extra luggage/equipment: +$10
  • Off-hours (before 7am / after 8pm): +$5
  • Bilingual service (EN/ES): included at no charge

7. Payments & Refunds

Payments are processed by Stripe. By booking you authorize Tascli to charge your payment method for the confirmed fare.

Refund Policy

  • Driver no-show (+15 min): Full refund
  • Ride not completed by driver: Full refund
  • Passenger cancels 24+ hrs before: Full refund
  • Passenger cancels 2–24 hrs before: 50% refund
  • Passenger cancels less than 2 hrs before: No refund
  • No-show at pickup after 10 min: No refund

Refund requests: [email protected] within 48 hours of the ride.

8. Cancellations
